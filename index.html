Our Hotel

rooftop swimming pool

Our exclusive rooftop pool is open year round, with sweeping views of Sydney Harbour. Please note the pool is heated during our autumn and winter months and there is no lifeguard on duty. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

the guest house

Our premium event space sits right on the water’s edge, with uninterrupted views of the Sydney Opera House. Space highlights include a private outdoor terrace, show kitchen, and floor-to-ceiling glass windows.

fitness center

Our fully equipped fitness center has a range of cardiovascular and weight trainning equipment. Over looking Dawns Point Park and Sydney Harbour.

gellary of australian art
